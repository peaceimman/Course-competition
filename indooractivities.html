Indoor Activities

Junkyard Golf Club
An alternative crazy golf experience situated in a vibrant colourful venue. Putt your way through towers of tyres, written-off cars and auto-junk. Great experience, good company and a fun challenge. What more could you ask for?
Location: Brick Lane, London

Oxygen Freejumping Acton
Are your kids a bit bouncier than you'd like? Embrace their spring at Oxygen Freejumping acton!Explore through 150 interconnected trampolines. You can even challenge your friends with volleybal and dodgeball. With plenty of activties the bouncing never stops.
Location: Acton, London

Clip n 'Climb
An exciting climbing experience you won't ever forget. If you enjoy climbing, you will love this. It combines mountain climbing with a theme park experience. It has 22 separate challenges-ranging form the jungle Gym, which involves commando style to the Speed Climbing Challenge. There's fun for everyone!
Location: Chelsea, London
