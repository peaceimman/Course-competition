Outdoor Activities

Go Ape Battersea Park
Go Ape Battersea is twice the height, length and scare factor of most other Go Ape courses. It is in the heart of Battersea Park, on the south bank of the River Thames. It's the perfect way to get your kids being active while not losing out on the fun factor!
Location: Battersea Park

Coram's Fields
Coram's Fields is a unique seven acre playground and park for children living in or visiting London. It is open access, provides a calm, safe and stimulating environment where children can play freely.
Location: Camden

Capital Karts
Whether you are looking to race for fun, arrange a kids party, have private tuition or even pursue a career in Motorsport, they have kids go karting packages to suit all occasions!
Location: Barking
